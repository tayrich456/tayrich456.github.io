My Family.

My immediate family consists of myself my mom, my dad, my brother, my sister, and my grandmother. My brother, Greyson, is actually my half brother; while my sister, Lauren, is my "full" sister. Contrary to the pictures below, people say that my sister, who is 3 years younger than me, could be considered my twin (I don't see it); seriously, people get us mixed up all the time. The little old lady in my family portrait is my grandma, or as we call her, Nannie. Don't let her age fool you, she's one of the most wild people I've ever met. Overall, my family seems pretty conventional, but I'd consider them to be anything but that.
